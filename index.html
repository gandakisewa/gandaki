Our Services

New Home Construction

Gandaki Nirman Sewa is a Nepalese construction company specializing in new building construction. They prioritize safety and quality control, and work closely with clients to meet their needs and preferences. Their services include site preparation, foundation work, framing, roofing, electrical and plumbing installations, interior and exterior finishes, and landscaping.

Home Additions

Gandaki Nirman Sewa offers top-quality home addition services to help you expand your living space and increase the value of your home. Our team of experts will work with you to design and build a new addition that seamlessly integrates with your existing home and meets your specific needs and requirements. Contact us today to get started.

Road Construction

Rigid pavement is a type of road construction using concrete slabs that are designed to distribute heavy loads and provide a durable, long-lasting surface for vehicles to travel on. This type of pavement is commonly used for highways, airports, and industrial areas. As a construction company, Gandaki Nirman Sewa can provide high-quality and efficient rigid pavement construction services to meet the needs of various infrastructure projects.

Road Project

Gandaki Nirman Sewa can efficiently design and construct durable rigid pavement for road construction projects. With our experienced team and commitment to quality, we can deliver safe and long-lasting driving surfaces on time and within budget.

Water supply project

Gandaki Nirman Sewa specializes in constructing water supply RV tanks of up to 500 cubic meters. Our team of experts can design and construct large-capacity tanks that are durable, reliable, and meet the specific needs of RV owners. We use high-quality materials and employ modern construction techniques to ensure that our tanks are long-lasting and easy to maintain. With our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, we can deliver water supply RV tanks that meet or exceed your expectations.

Pipe line

Gandaki Nirman Sewa provides comprehensive services for water supply pipeline projects in Nepal. We specialize in project planning, design, construction, and maintenance of water supply pipelines for communities, industries, and households. Our services include site preparation, pipeline installation, and testing, as well as the construction of water storage tanks and pump stations. With our commitment to quality and safety, we ensure that our water supply pipeline projects meet or exceed the standards and expectations of our clients.
